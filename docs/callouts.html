Callouts[ Variable Reference ]

A callout provides a simple way to draw attention to information. It is an element with the class .callout applied.

Basic Callout

Important information!

Jellyfish have no brain, heart, bones or eyes. They are made up of a smooth, bag-like body and tentacles armed with tiny, stinging cells.

Cool - tell me more!

Colors

Provide more context to callouts by using the modifiers .primary, .secondary, .success, .warning or .error.
This is a primary call out

Despite their name, jellyfish aren't really a type of fish. Fish are vertebrates that live in water and breathe through their gills. Jellyfish, on the other hand, are invertebrates, meaning they have no backbone and they absorb oxygen from water through membranes.
